Welcome to the TestGrid Assessment and Development Centre's Sample Testing Room

The TestGrid online Assessment and Development Centre offers some of Australia's most valid and reliable measures of ability and personality. They are used by employers to flesh out candidate qualifications, experience, abilities, preferences and likely on the job performance. This means that candidates can be compared fairly to the job description and ranked as fairly as possible in order of likely on the job success.

Employers use TestGrid and similar services in the selection of personnel to make the process more complete and fairer. This is because researchers agree that in jobs requiring high intelligence or specialist aptitudes (such as mechanical reasoning) or particular personality traits such as the optimism and achievement drive needed in sales, the kind of testing offered by TestGrid provides the greatest predictor of on the job success. And wouldn't you want any employer to place you in a job in which you have the highest likelihood of success?
